Dog Food

Description

This is one of my favorites! Quick and easy with only a few ingredients. I call it dog food because I know people that will fix this up for their pups. Don't let the name scare you away, it tastes better than it sounds.

With only some white rice, ground beef, and a few different seasonings you'll be on your way to having a delicious, nutritious, filling, and easy to make meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • White or brown rice
  • Egg (optional)
  • Stir fry seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat ground beef over medium heat until browned
  2. While ground beef is cooking, stir in seasonings and soy sauce
  3. Boil water and cook rice
  4. Cook a fried egg
  5. When all is finished, layer ground beef over a serving of rice and top it off with your fried egg
  6. Drizzle soy sauce overtop to finish
